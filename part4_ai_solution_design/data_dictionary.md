# Part 4 Dataset: AI Solution Design Reference Data

## Files
- `ai_usecase_reference_catalog.csv`
- `business_kpi_sample.csv`

## Purpose
These files are optional support files for the business-solution-design part. Students may use them to choose a domain, define an AI task type, recommend a model, and connect the AI solution to business metrics.

## Suggested Student Tasks
- Select one domain from the catalog
- Define the business problem clearly
- Identify data requirements and model type
- Use the KPI sample to discuss measurable business impact
- Discuss responsible AI risks and mitigation
